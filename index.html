PART 2: SENTENCE COMPLETION

10-ITEM TEST | PASSING SCORE: 8

Direction: In each of the following sentences, there is a blank space indicating that a word or phrase has been omitted. From among the suggested answers, choose the word or phrase that best fits in the sentence to make it grammatically correct.

1. The teacher insists that he ______ the paper before the deadline.

2. It is essential that he ______ early to prepare for the meeting.

3. I wish I ______ taller so I could play basketball more competitively.

4. It’s important that she ______ aware of the rules when filling out the application.

5. The manager demanded that the employee ______ due to repeated violations.

6. I would have helped if I ______ there to see what was going on.

7. It’s necessary that she ______ the interview if she wants the job.

8. If you ______ harder, you would pass the exam easily.

9. I suggest that she ______ more carefully for the upcoming presentation.

10. It’s required that all students ______ the lecture on academic integrity.

Answer Explanations:

  1. C. submit
    Triggered by "insists" → use base form for subjunctive.
  2. C. arrive
    "It is essential that" requires subjunctive → base form.
  3. B. were
    Contrary-to-fact wish → use "were" for all subjects.
  4. B. be
    "Important that" → subjunctive mood → base form "be".
  5. A. resign
    "Demanded that" → subjunctive base form "resign".
  6. C. had been
    Third conditional → If + past perfect + would have...
  7. A. not miss
    Subjunctive + negative → "not" + base verb.
  8. D. studied
    Second conditional → If + past simple + would...
  9. B. prepare
    "Suggest that" → subjunctive → base verb.
  10. C. attend
    "Required that" → subjunctive → base verb.
