CIVIL SERVICE EXAM PRACTICE TEST

CREATED BY: CSE LEARNING ESSENTIALS

SECTION 1: VERBAL ABILITY

PART 5: PARAGRAPH ORGANIZATION

PRACTICE TEST 1 | 5-ITEM TEST | PASSING SCORE: 4

Direction: Determine which may be the best order in which the given sentences should be arranged to result in a well-organized paragraph.

1. Given sentences:
I. Critics argue that it could negatively affect small businesses.
II. A new tax policy has been proposed to increase government revenue.
III. The policy includes higher taxes for online sellers and freelancers.
IV. Supporters believe it will help fund public services and reduce debt.

2. Given sentences:
I. Many schools have begun integrating AI tools into their teaching strategies.
II. Some educators worry that over-reliance on technology may reduce critical thinking skills.
III. These innovations are intended to enhance learning and personalize education.
IV. The debate continues over the best way to balance traditional methods with modern tools.

3. Given sentences:
I. The government announced plans to invest in renewable energy projects nationwide.
II. This shift is expected to reduce the country’s dependence on imported fossil fuels.
III. Environmental groups have praised the initiative as a step toward sustainability.
IV. However, critics argue that implementation may be delayed due to budget constraints.

4. Given sentences:
I. Online learning platforms saw a surge in users during the pandemic.
II. Students and professionals turned to these platforms to continue their education remotely.
III. While the trend has declined slightly post-pandemic, interest in flexible learning remains strong.
IV. Experts believe this marks a permanent shift in how people approach education.

5. Given sentences:
I. Smartphone addiction is becoming a growing concern among parents and educators.
II. Some studies link excessive screen time to sleep problems and reduced attention spans.
III. Experts recommend setting screen time limits and encouraging outdoor activities.
IV. The goal is to promote healthier digital habits in children and teens.

Answer Explanations:

  1. B. II-III-I-IV
    Introduces policy → gives detail → shows criticism → ends with support.
  2. A. I-III-II-IV
    Begins with AI in schools → explains benefit → presents worry → ends with ongoing debate.
  3. D. I-II-III-IV
    Starts with gov’t action → expected benefit → praise → concern about implementation.
  4. A. I-II-III-IV
    Chronological order from surge → reasons → updates → expert view.
  5. C. I-II-IV-III
    Problem → evidence → goal → solution.
