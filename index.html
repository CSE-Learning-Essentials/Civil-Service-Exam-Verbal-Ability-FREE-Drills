PART 2: SENTENCE COMPLETION

10-ITEM TEST | PASSING SCORE: 8

Direction: In each of the following sentences, there is a blank space indicating that a word or phrase has been omitted. From among the suggested answers, choose the word or phrase that best fits in the sentence to make it grammatically correct.

1. We have just interviewed an applicant ____ the committee believes is best qualified for the position.

2. Mrs. White was delighted that the Fernandez family had invited John and ____ to ____ party.

3. Maybe we’re not as rich as ____, but I bet we’re a lot ____.

4. Tell ____ is waiting to come in.

5. They were talking about ____ before the meeting started.

6. No one but ____ ever made a perfect score on the test.

7. Was it ____ ____ called you last night?

8. ____ is the woman ____, we believe, will be our next governor.

9. When do you think that Joy and ____ should leave?

10. Ask ____ and Bobby for their opinions, as ____ are more knowledgeable than ____.
